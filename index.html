Hello! I'm Ciril, a passionate Robotics and Automation engineer-in-training based in Coimbatore. This curiosity led me to the fascinating field of robotics, where I explore the intersection of hardware, software, mathematics, and physics.

I'm constantly learning, experimenting, and seeking opportunities to contribute to innovative projects in automation and robotics.
